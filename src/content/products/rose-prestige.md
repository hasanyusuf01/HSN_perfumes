Rose Prestige begins with a playful overture of lychee and pink pepper before revealing its true heart — a magnificent dual-rose accord blending the honeyed warmth of Bulgarian rose with the more delicate, lush Turkish variety, softened by white peony. The finish is powdery and ethereal, with soft cedar providing structure beneath a cloud of white musk and iris.

This is the definitive rose perfume for those who understand that roses are not one note, but a symphony.

**Longevity:** 8–10 hours  
**Sillage:** Moderate  
**Occasion:** Daytime, Formal, Gifting
