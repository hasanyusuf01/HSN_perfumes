Santal Lumière is liquid warmth. The name means "sandalwood light" in French, and this fragrance truly glows from within. A refined opening of bergamot, pink pepper, and green cardamom gives way to the star ingredient — premium Australian sandalwood, renowned for its creamy, milky-smooth character that surpasses all other varieties. Iris adds a cool powdery elegance before the base reveals its luxurious vanilla bourbon and amber warmth.

This is the fragrance you reach for when you want to feel beautiful without effort.

**Longevity:** 10–12 hours  
**Sillage:** Moderate  
**Occasion:** All-Season, Work, Casual Luxury
