Amber Noir is the fragrance of midnight hours and candlelit rooms. An opening of sharp black pepper and cool bergamot transitions into a rich heart of leather and patchouli, grounded by the deep, resinous warmth of labdanum. The base is a masterwork — amber, vanilla, and benzoin weaving together into something that feels like velvet against the skin.

For evenings when you want to be remembered long after you've left the room.
