Jasmine Essence captures that singular moment just after dusk when jasmine flowers release their most intoxicating scent into the warm night air. Bright neroli and mandarin introduce the fragrance before yielding to an opulent jasmine absolute — rich, creamy, and slightly animalic in the way that true jasmine absolute always is. Tuberose adds drama, orange blossom adds delicacy, and the whole composition settles on a clean, mossy base of vetiver and light woods.

A celebration of nature's most bewitching bloom.

**Longevity:** 8–10 hours  
**Sillage:** Moderate to Heavy  
**Occasion:** Evening, Summer, Date Night
